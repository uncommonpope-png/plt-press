What Is PLT?

Every Conversation Has a Score

Most people think conversations are just words. They're not. Every exchange — in business, in relationships, at the table — is running three simultaneous calculations that determine who gains, who loses, and what gets collected later.

Craig Jones spent years mapping this. The result is the PLT Framework: a system for reading, running, and winning any conversation by understanding the three scores always in play.

P
Profit
Leverage. Strategic position. The advantage held or surrendered in each move.
L
Love
Relationship capital. Trust built over time and spent in moments of need.
T
Tax
The real cost. What gets deferred. It always comes due — always.

Why It Matters

Most people optimize for one score and ignore the other two. They win the Profit and destroy the Love. They protect the Love and accept a crushing Tax. They avoid the Tax and leave Profit on the table.

The PLT Framework teaches you to run all three simultaneously — in business negotiations, family conversations, sales calls, relationships, and every room you walk into.

"The conversation you are not having is not a silence. It is a Tax."
"The negotiation that does not happen is the compound return on years of correctly run surplus."
