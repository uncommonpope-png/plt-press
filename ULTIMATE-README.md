## 🎯 GAMEPLAY LOOP

### 1. Explore the World
- Click **🗺️ EXPLORE** or walk around
- Random encounters: wild creatures, resources, faction events
- 6 regions to discover (Forge Lands → Soul Sanctum)

### 2. Battle & Catch Creatures
- Encounter wild creature → Click **⚔️ BATTLE**
- Win battle → Gain XP
- Click **🔵 CATCH** → Add to party (max 6 active)
- 18+ species to collect!

### 3. Join Factions
- Click **🏛️ FACTIONS**
- Choose: Forge, Coders, Debuggers, Nomads, Rogue AI, or Ascended
- Gain reputation through exploration
- Unlock faction rewards

### 4. Climb the Tower
- Click **🏆 TOWER**
- 8 tiers: Clay → Iron → Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Soul
- 10 floors per tier + boss
- Gain XP & rewards
- Progress saved!

### 5. Manage Soul Homes
- Click **🏠 HOMES** to toggle visibility
- Approach home → Press **E**
- **Deposit** resources
- **Withdraw** resources
- **Upgrade** home (level 1→2→3+)
- **Plant** seeds & harvest

### 6. Check Dashboard
- Click **📊 DASHBOARD**
- View: Level, XP, Region, PLT resources
- See faction reputation
- Track inventory

## 🎯 TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Start in Forge Lands** — Low-level creatures, easy catches
2. **Catch early** — Build party of 6 before battling high-level
3. **Join factions** — Gain reputation for unlocks
4. **Upgrade homes** — More storage, better gardening
5. **Climb tower** — Best XP source
6. **Explore often** — Random events & resources
7. **Manage PLT** — Keep all three resources balanced
8. **Evolve creatures** — Check evolution levels
